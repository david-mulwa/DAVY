About us

Who We Are?

We are dedicated to making the life of car seekers easier

We are located in Nairobi, Kenya and other kenyan major cities

at better prices and deals without being exploited

We aim to cover generations of anything related to car revolution to serve you better and hence we are David and Sons .

Our online platform, David and Sons, has been designed to be extremely user-friendly
for you to use and find out the right vehicle without being an automotive enthusiast
or having any prior knowledge relating to cars.

Why Us?

  • Compare brands as well as prices to get the best deal
  • Connect with all the major automotive suppliers through a singular online platform
  • Save time and energy by eliminating the need to shop around individual supplier sites
  • Acquire any vehicle-related information instantly via same online platform

How It Works?

We have devoted ourselves to addressing the needs of car owners.

Using our online platform, you will be able to:

  • Choose the vehicle you actually need
  • see the price of the vehicle
  • able to buy the product and fill in the logbook which shall be delivered via post office to you

Our Vision

“To empower car owners.”

In response to the daily challenges faced by car owners all over Kenya,
we would like to empower them through this online platform which is truly customer centric.

The deliberate user-friendliness of this online tool will finally enable
car owners to negate the traditional challenges they have been subjected to in the past.

Our Mission

“We want to become the most trusted and widely accepted online tool by car owners.”

We aim to earn the trust of car owners by providing them the arsenal they
desperately need to alleviate the difficulties they have been facing prior to the inception of David and Sons.

We would continuously strive towards innovation and make this online platform
stronger to increase transparency for car owners and earn their unyielding loyalty.
